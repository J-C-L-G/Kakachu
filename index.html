Instructions

Creating Notes

  1. Click on the "Create Note" button and provide a title and message.

Deleting Notes

  1. Drag the Note that you want to delete and Drop it into the trash bin.

Modify a Note

  1. DoubleClick in the upper-right corner of the note to enter edit mode.
  2. Click in the content and enter the new information
  3. DoubleClick in the bottom-right corner of the note to exit edit mode.

Searching for content

  1. Write a word that you are searching for in a note and click "Search".
  2. Once done clean the search field and click "Search" again to bring all the notes again.

Re-Arranging the Notes

  1. Drag and Drop, Simple!.
